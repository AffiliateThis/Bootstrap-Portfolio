About Me
I am Chris Kautz-Scanavy and I am training to become a junior full stack developer. I have done quite a few things since graduating college amost 19 years ago. After college I moved to Richmond as that is where my band was located and Richmond had a good music scene in the early 2000's. Playing doesn't pay the bills, so I became a beerman. Now that was fun, driving a big ass beer truck with 60 kegs plus, selling and delivering them. I mean I was the beerman... then my back said, "screw you, your beerman days are over. Time to do something else."

I was a sub-teacher briefly and this was while recovering from a back injury and a rebuilt knee. Naturally, it was time to do outside MRO sales, nuts bolts, mechanical related stuff, and industrial chemicals. Upon further reflection and experience, outside sales in the MRO industry didn't seem like a great a fit for me. Most of these products could be purchased locally from the a hardware store. This is when I decided to join Corporate America and work in downtown Richmond doing inside sales for a car dealer lead generation company. I eventually made a transition while at the same company to the Marketing dept at to manage their lead acquisition and generation. Since that point, I have been in the online marketing industry for 14 years. That is a long time, and it is now time for me to build upon the knowledge and experience I have gainged and really grow my professional skillsets and become a full stack developer. Bam! Like I dropped a mic, well not really.
